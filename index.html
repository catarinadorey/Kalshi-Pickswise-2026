What is Kalshi?

Platform Identity

  • Regulated Prediction Market: An American platform for trading on real-world outcomes.
  • Financial Model: Operates as a Designated Contract Market (DCM), supervised by the CFTC.
  • Legal Status: It is a financial exchange for derivatives, not a traditional online casino.
US Federal Regulation

Product Mechanics

  • Binary Contracts: Users trade "Yes" or "No" on events like inflation, elections, and weather.
  • Payout Structure: Contracts pay a fixed value (usually $1) if the result is correct, or $0 if not.
  • Market Pricing: Prices reflect the market's estimated probability of an event.

Access & Restrictions

Strict Geographical Boundaries

Currently, Kalshi is restricted mainly to United States residents. To trade, users must meet these criteria:

  • Provide a valid US residential address.
  • Complete identity verification (KYC) under US federal regulations.
  • Link compatible US bank accounts.
  • Note: Using VPNs or similar tools is not a valid or legal alternative.
No Global Access Without Local Approval

What is Kalshi?

Platform Identity

  • Regulated Prediction Market: An American platform for trading on real-world outcomes[cite: 2].
  • Financial Model: Operates as a Designated Contract Market (DCM), supervised by the CFTC[cite: 8].
  • Legal Status: It is a financial exchange for derivatives, not a traditional casino[cite: 10].
US Federal Regulation

Product Mechanics

  • Binary Contracts: Users trade "Yes" or "No" on events like inflation or elections[cite: 6].
  • Payout Structure: Contracts pay a fixed value ($1) if correct, or $0 if incorrect[cite: 14, 15].
  • Market Pricing: Prices reflect the market's estimated probability of an event[cite: 13].

Access & Restrictions

Strict Geographical Boundaries

  • US Residents Only: Users must provide a US residential address and identity proof[cite: 22, 23].
  • KYC Verification: Identity must be verified under US federal regulations (CFTC)[cite: 24].
  • No Workarounds: Using VPNs is not a valid or legal way to access the platform[cite: 31, 32].
